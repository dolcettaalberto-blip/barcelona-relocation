Fit score = school access ×3 + green + bike + safety − weekly commute hours (round trips, 3 office days) ×1.2. Higher is better. Out-of-city areas carry no editorial ratings, so their score reflects commute only.

Data as of June 2026, asking prices. Compiled from idealista district averages (May 2026), city cycling/parks data and school fee schedules. Asking prices, not closed prices.

  • Prices are asking averages per district; renovated/penthouse stock near top schools trades above them, older stock below. Validate any shortlist with recent idealista comparables before offers.
  • Green / bike / school-access / safety are editorial 1–5 scores derived from the research narrative, not official indices.
  • Fee ranges are 2026 headline tuition; the 25–35% uplift for extras (enrolment, lunch, bus, uniforms, exams) is a rule of thumb.
  • Commute minutes are editorial door-to-door estimates (mixed mode, off-peak-ish), not routed times.
  • Maresme coast, Sitges/Castelldefels and other out-of-city €/m² figures are estimates — verify on idealista before trusting the budget filter there.
  • Rent figures are rough 2026 asking estimates; Catalonia's rent-cap zoning distorts asking vs achievable — verify per listing.
  • State schools are excluded by design; the comparison covers international and bilingual private options only.
